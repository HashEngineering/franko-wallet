Have your Frankos always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Franko Wallet is the first mobile Franko app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Franko amount in FRK and mFRK.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Franko via NFC, QR-codes or Franko URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Franko addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Franko balance.

You can find more about Franko at
http://www.frankos.org
